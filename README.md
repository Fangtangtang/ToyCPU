# ToyCPU
实现简易功能的一级流水CPU。

## Instruction Set
### add
```text
|     ADD     | Add                                      |

31           25 24         20 19         15 14 12 11          7 6       0
+--------------+-------------+-------------+-----+-------------+---------+
|   0000000    |     rs2     |     rs1     | 000 |     rd      | 0110011 | ADD

```

```text
|    ADDI     | Add Immediate                            |

31           25 24         20 19         15 14 12 11          7 6       0
+--------------+-------------+-------------+-----+-------------+---------+
|         imm[11:0]          |     rs1     | 000 |     rd      | 0010011 | ADDI


```

### branch
```text
|     BEQ     | Branch if Equal                          |

31           25 24         20 19         15 14 12 11          7 6       0
+--------------+-------------+-------------+-----+-------------+---------+
| imm[12|10:5] |     rs2     |     rs1     | 000 | imm[4:1|11] | 1100011 | BEQ

```

```text
|     LW      | Load Word                                |

+--------------+-------------+-------------+-----+-------------+---------+
|         imm[11:0]          |     rs1     | 010 |     rd      | 0000011 | LW

```
